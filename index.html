About

Budding front end web developer, my passion for beautiful designs led me there.
I am passionate about learning and i love to Fix it if it's not working.

As a front end web developer, I have always aspired to use the internet to create things. I realised that the internet is a powerful tool capable of solving any problem and I am always fascinated about designs and the functionality of websites - how they could be improved and what makes them sucessful.

I attended Obafemi Awolowo University in Ile-Ife where I studied Estate Management. I practised for about 2 years before I decided to quit and learn programming. You can mail me here if you want more information about this decision.

I am currently learning and improving on Javascript frameworks such as Vue Js and React. As a person I am organized, punctual and a fast learner. I enjoy problem solving, troubleshooting and motivating myself, which I feel plays a key part in learning how to use programming languages and creating websites. I also enjoy working in a group or independently; I have done this sucessfully in previous jobs. I am confident these characteristics will help me excel in this chosen field and provide me with the skills required to be successful within the web development industry.
